Our Main Focus

  • Property Management

    We offer a full scope of Property Management services for our clients, Tenants as well as Property Owners. We manage some of the areas most desirable rentals. Our progressive staff understands that consistently working to improve the quality of service and attention to detail are the best ways to provide a comfortable experience for the people who have chosen our rentals. Our tenants feel good knowing that they will be able to reach a qualified employee who will promptly address any maintenance or property issues as it relates to their safety or the integrity of the property they are renting. This is one of the best property management in kenya.

  • Facilities Management

    Facilities Management Service is a specialized area of property management. It entails management of a property’s common services, which includes but is not limited to the following Street parking & maintenance Access control Landscaping of common areas Sewer System Operation Management Pest Control Generator Operation & Maintenance Lift Maintenance Electric Fence Maintenance Payment of Land Rates and Rents Service Charge Billing.
